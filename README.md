## 🌟 About

DentalCare is a professional website designed for dental clinics to showcase their services, introduce their team, and allow patients to easily book appointments. The website features a clean, modern design with a focus on user experience and accessibility.

## ✨ Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI/UX**: Clean and professional design with smooth animations
- **Multiple Pages**: Home, Services, and Contact pages
- **Interactive Forms**: Contact and appointment booking forms with validation
- **Mobile Navigation**: Hamburger menu for mobile devices
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Smooth navigation between sections
- **Service Showcase**: Detailed information about dental services
- **Team Section**: Introduction to dental professionals
- **Testimonials**: Patient reviews and feedback
- **Emergency Contact**: Prominent display of emergency contact information
- **Google Maps Integration**: Location map on contact page
